Responsive web design helps designers factor in all of these different devices and screen resolutions without having to design a site for every, single device.

As someone who does at least half of her browsing on a smart phone, I know the frustration of horrible design. Having to flip my phone around trying to get the best viewing experience, and then having to scroll endlessly, only to end up frustrated and just leaving the site. In business, having a user leave your site without making a purchase, or getting the information they need can make or break you. We all need responsive web design to make our lives somewhat easier when designing for the past, present, and future technology.
